Translation Policy

01. Use UTF-8 as encoding
e.g. <?xml version = '1.0' encoding = 'utf-8'?>

02. Surround English words with spaces
e.g. Fink で HTTP プロキシ ...

03. one sentence = one line

04. Leave internal links at they are

05. Use polite expression for normal lines.  Use descriptive if in lists.
XXX です。XXX して下さい。
1. XXX する。
