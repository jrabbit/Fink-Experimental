Fink とは

MacOSX用のパッケージ管理システムです。
    MacOSX用パッケージ管理システム
  • fink - /sw like - best is unstable tree - binaries available - lots of ports
  • macports - /opt like - status as work-in-progress - no binaries - lots of ports
  • netBSD's pkgsrc - /usr/pkg like - active group on portability, status as stable - no binaries - common ports
  • pkgin - an attempt to manage pkg binaries
  • EasyPackages - 日本人作成

Fink を使う 10 の理由

Please note that I am not saying these against any other package management system, unless it is explicitly stated.

  1. No worry about dependency - For most other things, I am an independent person. I work, I wash my clothes, go to bathroom on my own, except for installing unix tools. When I install Kate, I need someone else to sort out all the dependencies.
  2. No worry about options - More than sometimes, I don't care options. I just want a standard one. So, pick the first variant.
  3. Binaries available I am proud that I am a student at Todai.
  4. /sw is safe - Fink installs almost everything in /sw. I sudo rm -rf /sw once in a few months to start from scratch. Some people claim that they don't like /sw, and I agree to some extent. But, I don't like /usr/local, either.
  5. Stay up-to-date - Fink's packages are too old? Just edit the .info files and build them! If it worked, Don't forget to tell the maintainer.
  6. Good documentation, well translated - I translate the documents, news, etc. into Japanese. So, the Japanese version is always up-to-date when I read.
  7. Unicode! - Most packages are unicode-aware, as long as the unicode is available.
  8. Fink is on Mac - Suppose you want to use Unix tools, such as LaTeX. Let's think this way: Mac or Ubuntu? Mac or *BSD? I am much happier with Mac.
  9. .info is a game Writing a .info is more like a game. The bigger a package, the stronger the enermy. You will spend one week or one month to fix one error. You cannot sleep before you beat the last boss.
  10. Lovely finch logo. OK, I admit that I've got only nine. This is just a makeup.
